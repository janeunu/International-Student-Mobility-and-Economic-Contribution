Colour & typography / Why / Avoid

Off-white ground, charcoal type, one accent for Australia and primary actions; sector palette colorblind-safe.

Works: Clear story order; badges trace back to Sheets 2–4; supervisor can see convergence.

Avoid: Four disconnected tabs with no copy glue; sensationalising concentration; implying rankings are strictly comparable.
